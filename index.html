Why Determined Detailing

Determined Detailing puts our best foot forward in taking great care of your vehicle. Policy


Thank you for taking the time to read the policy page! I know you are eager to schedule your appointment, but there's some important information you need to know. When you schedule a time slot, a non-refundable deposit of $20 must be paid 48 hours prior to your scheduled time slot or else your appointment will be cancelled. This deposit must be paid through Cashapp or Venmo with the description: "For appointment date".

For days when the forcast shows rain, exterior details will have to be rescheduled and interior details can only be done if a garage space is available. Your phone number is required while scheduling and it is likely we will contact you about further details.



Extra Information


Time durations of each package are currently being experimented with and can be viewed on Calendly after you press "Schedule". Please be courteous for the sake of this learning curve.

Distance of details must not exceed 25 miles of Gasport, NY. Depending on circumstances, exceptions could be made.

By scheduling an appointment, you agree to have your car photographed before and after the detailing process and damages to vehicle or goods of any nature are not responsible by Determined Detailing.
